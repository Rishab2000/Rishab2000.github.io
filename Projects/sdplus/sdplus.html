Understanding our user's problems
After the initial feedback session, we validated the usability of the app through interviews. Through the interviewees, we learned that they found the current usability & functionality insufficient for their needs.

Design Challenge
How might we refocus and structurize the product's experience.
Approach
Defining user stages (pre-sale to post-sale)
The first thing we took up was organizing our roadmap, on a high level, by user stages and calling it Experiences. From start to finish we mapped five distinct stages our users would pass through.





Identifying touchpoints
We now started defining our user interactions (from start to finish) by describing touch points our users would use to interact with our organization.





Synthesizing insights
While gathering insights we focused on answering three main questions -

  • What users are thinking at that time?
  • What users are feeling at that time?
  • Are there any points of friction at that time?




Research
Next plan of action was to conduct research by communicating directly with the users. For the research we laid down key points we wanted to determine:

  • What is the goal our user wants to achieve?
  • What are their feelings towards the current process in place? Does it meet their expectations?
  • What touchpoints and steps does the user take in each phase to achieve their goal? How do they find and interact with these necessary touchpoints?
  • What are their emotions and how do they feel as they interact with each touchpoint? Why?
  • Is there any friction as they pass through different customer phases?
  • How easy is the trasnition between each phase?
